FC26 Skills Guide

Dribbling Skills

  • Rainbow Flick R1ForwardO – Beat defenders 1v1
  • Stepovers R1Right Stick – Fake moves to confuse opponents
  • Ball Roll Right StickForward – Controlled dribble
  • Heel Flick R1OLeft Stick – Escape tight marking
  • Roulette / 360 Spin Right StickRotate – Advanced bypass
  • Drag Back Flick R2Left Stick Down – Move out of space
  • Body Feint Left Stick Left/Right – Fake defender direction

Attacking Skills

  • Chip Goal L1O – Lift the ball over the keeper
  • Finesse Shot R1O – Curved accurate shot
  • Fake Pass SquareX – Trick defenders
  • Body Feint Shot Left StickRightO – Mislead goalkeeper

Defending Skills

  • Standing Tackle O – Stop attacker
  • Slide Tackle Square – Time it perfectly
  • Jockey L2 – Control space
  • Push/Pull R1 – Unbalance attacker
  • Intercept Triangle – Read passes early

Goalkeeping Skills

  • Diving Save Right Stick + O – Position keeper correctly
  • One-on-One L1O – Anticipate shooter
  • Goal Kick Circle – Long accurate pass
  • Quick Throw Triangle – Start counter attack

Hidden / Rare Skills

  • Drag Back Elastico R1L2Right Stick – Confuse advanced players
  • Double Rainbow Flick R1Forward + O x2 – Ultra rare move
  • Heel Chop Right Stick + L1 + O – Escape tight marking
