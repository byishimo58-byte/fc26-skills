FC26 Skill Moves

All skills to master the game! From attacking, defending, goalkeeping, to secret pro moves.

Attacking Skills

  • Rainbow Flick – Beat 1v1 defenders.
  • Elastico – Quick change of direction near the box.
  • Stepovers – Fake moves to confuse opponents.
  • Ball Roll – Controlled dribble for positioning.
  • Heel Flick – Surprise defenders with quick flicks.
  • Roulette / 360 Spin – Advanced move to bypass multiple defenders.
  • Drag Back Flick – Move quickly away from tight spaces.
Pro Tip: Combine Stepovers + Drag Back Flick to completely break your opponent’s defense!

Defending Skills

  • Standing Tackle – Basic defender control.
  • Slide Tackle – Time it right to stop dangerous attacks.
  • Jockey – Control space and anticipate opponent moves.
  • Push & Pull – Subtle nudges to unbalance attackers.
  • Intercept – Read passes and cut the attack early.
Pro Tip: Use Jockey + Intercept together to dominate midfield defense!

Goalkeeping Tips

  • Diving – Always position your keeper correctly for saves.
  • One-on-One – Stay on your feet, anticipate the shooter.
  • Goal Kick – Launch accurate long passes to teammates.
  • Quick Throw – Start counterattacks faster than your opponent.
Pro Tip: Use keeper positioning + quick throws to create fast break goals!

Rare / Hidden Skills

  • Drag Back Elastico – Confuses high-level opponents.
  • Fake Pass + Ball Roll – Perfect for cutting inside defenders.
  • Reverse Roulette – Very rare but deadly if mastered.
  • Heel Chop – Advanced skill to escape tight marking.
  • Double Rainbow Flick – Ultra rare move for top players.
Pro Tip: Practicing hidden skills gives you an edge against everyone online!
