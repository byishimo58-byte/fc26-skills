FC26 Skill Moves

All skills to master the game! From attacking, defending, goalkeeping, to secret pro moves.

Attacking Skills

  • Rainbow Flick – Beat defenders 1v1.
  • Elastico – Quick direction change near the box.
  • Stepovers – Fake moves to confuse opponents.
  • Ball Roll – Controlled dribble for positioning.
  • Heel Flick – Surprise defenders quickly.
  • Roulette / 360 Spin – Advanced move to escape pressure.
  • Drag Back Flick – Escape tight spaces fast.
Pro Tip: Combine Stepovers + Drag Back Flick to completely break your opponent’s defense!

Defending Skills

  • Standing Tackle – Basic defender control.
  • Slide Tackle – Perfect timing stops attacks.
  • Jockey – Control space and movement.
  • Push & Pull – Subtle body control defense.
  • Intercept – Cut passes early.
Pro Tip: Use Jockey + Intercept together to dominate midfield defense!

Goalkeeping Tips

  • Diving – Position correctly before shots.
  • One-on-One – Stay calm and wait for the shot.
  • Goal Kick – Accurate long distribution.
  • Quick Throw – Start fast counterattacks.
Pro Tip: Quick throws after a save create easy counter goals!

Rare / Hidden Skills

  • Drag Back Elastico – Confuses high-level players.
  • Fake Pass + Ball Roll – Perfect inside cut move.
  • Reverse Roulette – Rare but deadly.
  • Heel Chop – Escape tight marking.
  • Double Rainbow Flick – Ultra rare skill.
Pro Tip: Hidden skills give you advantage against normal players!
